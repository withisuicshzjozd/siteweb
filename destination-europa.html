03 Destination

Europa

The smallest of the four Galilean moons orbiting Jupiter, Europa is a winter lover’s dream. With an icy surface, it’s perfect for a bit of ice skating, curling, hockey, or simple relaxation in your snug wintery cabin.

Distance from earth

628 mil. km

Est. travel time

3 years


Potential for Life:The presence of liquid water, essential chemical elements,
and energy sources from tidal heating has led scientists to speculate about the possibility
of life on Europa. Future missions, like the Europa Clipper by NASA, aim to explore this moon
further and search for signs of habitability and potential life.


Missions to Europa: Several spacecraft have visited or observed Europa, including
the Galileo spacecraft in the 1990s, which provided valuable data about its surface and subsurface.
NASA's upcoming Europa Clipper mission, planned for launch in the 2020s, will conduct detailed investigations
of Europa's ocean, composition, and potential habitability
