02 Destination

Mars

Embark on a thrilling adventure to Mars and experience the wonders of the red planet. Explore its rocky landscapes, ancient volcanoes, and search for signs of extraterrestrial life. Discover the mysteries of Mars while enjoying breathtaking views of the universe.

Distance from earth:

54.6 million km

Est. travel time

6-9 months


Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun a dusty, cold, desert world with a very atmosphere.
Mars is also a dynamic planet with seasons, polar ice caps, canyons,extinct volcanoes,
and evidence that it was even more active in the past.


Possibility of Life: Scientists have been intrigued by the possibility of past or present life
on Mars. Evidence of liquid water in the past, the presence of organic compounds, and the potential
for subsurface habitats make Mars an important target in the search for extraterrestrial life.
